# Faraja Cancer Support Trust – Patient Survey Analysis

This repository contains the complete Excel-based analysis of patient feedback survey data for **Faraja Cancer Support Trust (FCST)**, a Kenyan organization providing holistic supportive care to cancer patients and their caregivers.

## Project Overview

The project involved:
- Cleaning raw survey response data
- Analyzing key metrics (demographics, cancer types, treatment stages, recommendation rates)
- Creating clear visualizations and summary findings

All work was performed in **Microsoft Excel**.

## Key Findings

- **Who we serve** - Predominantly women with breast cancer (aged 40–60s), plus smaller groups with prostate, colorectal, cervical, and other cancers.
- **When we support** - Services reach patients both during active treatment and in post-treatment follow-up, providing continuity of care.
- **Impact & satisfaction** - Over 90% of respondents across all cancer types and treatment stages say they would recommend Faraja’s supportive care services, indicating very high perceived value and satisfaction.

## Repository Contents

- `Raw_Data/`              – Original survey responses (anonymized)
- `Cleaned_Data.xlsx`      – Processed and cleaned dataset
- `Analysis_Report.xlsx`   – Main workbook with calculations, pivot tables, charts, and summary findings
- `Visuals/`               – (Optional) Exported charts and figures
- `Key_Insights_Summary.md` – Concise summary of main results

## Tools & Technologies

- Microsoft Excel (data cleaning, pivot tables, charts, conditional formatting)
- No external scripting or BI tools used

## Purpose

This analysis helps Faraja understand its reach, service delivery across the cancer journey, and patient satisfaction, supporting evidence-based improvements in cancer supportive care in Kenya.
